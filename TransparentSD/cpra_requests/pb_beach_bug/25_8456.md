# Request 25-8456

Subject: CPRA Request -- Termination of Pacific Beach "Beach Bug"
Shuttle Program

**To the Records Coordinator:**

Pursuant to the California Public Records Act (Gov. Code §§ 7920.000 et
seq.) and Article I, § 3(b) of the California Constitution, the San
Diego Transparency Project requests the following public records:

**Date Range:** January 1, 2024 -- present

If no records exist within this date range, please confirm that in your
response.

**Records Requested**

1.  All memoranda, emails, meeting notes, or correspondence reflecting
    or authorizing the decision to terminate, suspend, or "sunset" the
    Pacific Beach "Beach Bug" community shuttle program.
2.  Records identifying the City department, official, or advisory body
    that made or approved this decision.
3.  Any analyses or internal communications describing reasons for
    ending the program (e.g., ridership, cost, or overlap with other
    mobility programs).

**Preferred Format**

-   Please provide responsive records in their native digital format
    where available, consistent with Gov. Code § 6253.9.

**Processing & Timelines**

-   Please provide a determination within 10 calendar days as required
    by law.
-   If an extension is necessary, please identify the statutory basis
    under Gov. Code § 7922.535(b) and provide an estimated completion
    date.
-   If any responsive records can be produced before the entire request
    is complete, please release those portions on a rolling basis as
    they become available.
-   Please produce responsive records on a **rolling basis** as they
    become available.

Assistance to Clarify/Narrow

-   If clarification would expedite processing, please contact us
    pursuant to Gov. Code § 7922.600 so we may assist in refining scope.

Withholdings & Redactions

-   For any withheld or redacted records, please provide:
    -   The specific statutory exemption relied upon for each.
    -   A description sufficient to identify the nature of the record
        (e.g., email chain, memo, report) and approximate volume (page
        count or number of records).
    -   An explanation of how the exemption applies, as required by Gov.
        Code § 7922.535(a).

    Please release all reasonably segregable non-exempt portions.

**Search Scope**

Please confirm which custodians and systems will be searched, including
any relevant staff, consultants, contractors, or advisory bodies
involved in planning, funding, or evaluating the Beach Bug program.

This includes, but is not limited to, departments or offices responsible
for parking management, transportation, community mobility,
sustainability, or budget oversight.

If any potentially relevant custodians or record systems are excluded
from the search, please identify them and provide the reason.

Thank you for your assistance and timely response.
