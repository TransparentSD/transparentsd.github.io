# Request 25-8457

Subject: CPRA Request -- Reallocation of Funds Formerly Budgeted for
Beach Bug Shuttle

**To the Records Coordinator:**

Pursuant to the California Public Records Act, please provide records
showing how funds previously budgeted for the Pacific Beach "Beach Bug"
shuttle were reallocated or repurposed.

**Date Range:** July 1, 2023 -- present

If no records exist within this date range, please confirm that in your
response.

**Records Requested**

1.  Budget revisions, journal entries, or transfer authorizations
    reflecting changes to the Pacific Beach Community Parking District
    (Fund 200813) or any other fund line item that had supported the
    Beach Bug program.
2.  Correspondence or memoranda between the **City Treasurer's Office**,
    **Transportation Department**, and **Sustainability & Mobility
    Department** regarding redistribution or alternative use of those
    revenues.
3.  Any updated five-year outlooks, Council reports, or staff
    communications describing the discontinuation of Beach Bug funding.
