# Request 25-8458

Subject: CPRA Request -- Pacific Beach Community Parking District
Records re Beach Bug

Under the California Public Records Act, please provide the following
records of the **Pacific Beach Community Parking District (CPD)**
pertaining to the Beach Bug shuttle program.

If no records exist within this date range, please confirm that in your
response.

1.  Minutes, meeting packets, and staff presentations from the Pacific

    (Transportation Department, Sustainability & Mobility, or Mayor's
